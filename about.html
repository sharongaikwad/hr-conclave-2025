About HR Leadership Conclave & Excellence Awards 2025

The HR Leadership Conclave & Excellence Awards 2025 is a premier gathering of HR professionals, CHROs, and industry leaders, designed to explore the evolving landscape of talent development, leadership challenges, and AI integration in HR. As AI continues to reshape the workforce, HR professionals must adapt to new opportunities and challenges, from redefining employee experiences to leveraging AI for recruitment, learning, and workforce agility. The event will feature thought-provoking panel discussions, interactive sessions, and exclusive networking opportunities, ensuring that attendees gain actionable insights to navigate the future of HR with confidence.

About Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET) & Pimpri Chinchwad University (PCU)

Established in 1990, Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET) is a visionary institution committed to providing value-based education across diverse disciplines. With 10 institutes across three campuses in Nigdi, Ravet, and Sate, PCET has transformed the lives of over 52,200 students.

Pimpri Chinchwad University (PCU), established under Govt. of Maharashtra Act No. V of 2023, is a leading university in Pune, offering a cutting-edge curriculum, world-class infrastructure, and strong industry collaborations. With a dedicated faculty and a future-focused approach, PCU nurtures future entrepreneurs, business leaders, and innovators, equipping them with the skills required for success in today’s dynamic world.

About HR Shapers

HR Shapers is the world’s largest HR networking group, founded by Ashish Gakrey, with a global presence across 50 locations in 25 countries. The platform connects HR professionals, thought leaders, and industry experts, fostering knowledge-sharing and collaboration. Ashish Gakrey has also founded More Than HR Global (2002) and previously served as President of HR Infotech Association (HRIA) for IT/ITES HR professionals (2012-2013). He has also been a two-time Executive Committee Member of the NHRD Mumbai Chapter. HR Shapers continues to drive HR innovation by providing valuable insights, discussions, and networking opportunities for professionals worldwide.
